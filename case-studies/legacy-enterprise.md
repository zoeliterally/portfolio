# Legacy Enterprise Systems

## Context

Earlier in my career, I worked in enterprise and telecom-adjacent environments with long-lived Oracle applications, reporting, network planning workflows, data analysis tools, and integration tasks.

## Work

- Maintained and extended Oracle Forms/Reports applications and PL/SQL logic.
- Worked on Java, Perl, shell, and database components around operational network and planning systems.
- Built and maintained data analysis tooling in Perl and SQL/MySQL.
- Contributed to SAP-adjacent monitoring/integration work and BPM proof-of-concepts.
- Supported development and operational environments across Windows, Linux, and Unix-style systems.

## Technologies

- Strong ability to reason about old-but-critical systems without dismissing their constraints.
- Good foundation for modernization work where the first step is understanding business logic already encoded in legacy applications.
- Practical experience bridging database-heavy systems, scripts, reporting, and newer application layers.
