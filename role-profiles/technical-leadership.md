# Technical Leadership

## Positioning

I lead best when leadership stays close to the work: prioritizing tickets, mentoring people, reviewing implementation, improving operational processes, and still owning key technical projects.

## Relevant Strengths

- Technical leadership for a small internal IT team.
- Mentoring apprentices and reviewing technical work.
- Ticket prioritization, handover discipline, recurring calls, and coordination around maintenance windows.
- Project ownership across infrastructure migration, platform work, automation, and internal tooling.
- Clear boundary: hands-on technical leadership and team coordination, not inflated budget or sales ownership.
- AI-assisted workflows for documentation, ticket clarity, mentoring material, review preparation, and structured decision support.

## What I Bring To A Team

- Calm prioritization under operational pressure.
- A builder's understanding of the systems being led.
- Documentation and mentoring habits that reduce single-person knowledge.
- Bias toward useful automation, not process for its own sake.
